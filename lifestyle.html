My Lifestyle

I enjoy a tech-inspired lifestyle focused on balance—coding in the mornings, sketching UI ideas during breaks, and winding down with a good book or music.

Food

Food fuels both my brain and creativity! I love exploring local Malaysian dishes like nasi lemak and tom yum, and I often experiment with healthy cooking on weekends.

Travel

Traveling gives me fresh design inspiration. I document street signs, café interiors, and city layouts from trips to Penang, KL, and beyond—always thinking from a UI/UX perspective.

Fitness

Keeping active helps me stay focused. I follow short home workouts and campus walks daily to keep energy levels up and my mind refreshed after long screen sessions.

Books

From UI/UX handbooks to motivational reads, books have shaped how I design and approach problems. My recent favorite is “Don't Make Me Think” by Steve Krug.
